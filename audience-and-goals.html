Audience and Goals

Defining the Audience

I split my audience into 3 groups: Contributors, Those in Need (of help from Barefoot Artists), and audience members that fit into both of those categories. One of the beauties of this organization's projects is that many of the people being helped decide to participate in the creation process and transform themselves along with the public space.

Goals

Overarching Goals

Client

  • Spread the power of transformative art through communities in need and build a greater sense of community
  • Attract both local and international contributors to work on transforming the environment
  • Involving children/students in exploring their artistic creativity and expression
  • Raising awareness of the situation in a certain area to attract donations and volunteers to help with multiple aspects of the community
  • Spark a movement for change within the community that will lead to a greater economy

Audience

  • Learn about the organization's meaning, projects; what and how they do what they do
  • Become a sponsor or donate towards a specific project
  • Find out ways to become involved or learn more about the organization through events and workshops nearby or through social media
  • Find out ways to become involved in projects through volunteering opportunities

Website

  • Spread a message about the importance of working directly with and for the community while creating inspiration through the power of art
  • Spread information about current and past projects, as well as the impact of each community project
  • Promote a place for sponsors and other members to give donations
  • Advertise events and workshops taking place in various communities

Specific Goals

User Goal Content/Action
Artists and Volunteers Learn about the organization, get involved, contribute to current projects A CTA that leads to a basic overview of the most important work and examples of projects that they have already accomplished("Learn More"), a volunteer information page (that is more up-to-date and informative), a donation page
Sponsors Donations, fund a project, find out credibility of organization A donation button onhome page, a clear display of awards and fellowships (organized and a button somewhere on home screen to lead to page), contact page specifically for larger organizations/organizations looking to sponsor or invite for a project
Organizations, schools, churches, artists Learn more about the work itself and community impact, find opportunities, look for ideas/inspiration, Volunteer page and contact info for smaller organizations or requests, separate page for schools and churches to spread their process so that others could create similar projects
