Design Challenge

Using a worksheet from the IDEO Human Centered Design Toolkit I first determined the unique design challenges that were facing this website.

What is the problem you are trying to solve?

The lack of visual heirarchy and organization that prevents users from learning
about the organization and making donations.

Take a stab at framing it as a design question.

How can Barefoot Artists promote their organization in a meaningful and
informational way?

Now state the ultimate impact you’re trying to have.

Further the purpose of the organization by creating a design that will spread information/awareness more efficiently to attract a larger audience.

What are some possible solutions to your problem?

Present the information of the website in a way that prioritizes the projects
alongside the communities that created them. Also creating more visual interest through hierarchy and better use of imagery.

Finally, write down some of the context and constraints that you’re facing.

Some of the constraints I'm facing are actually a good thing: there is a lot of information about this organization and its projects, so it will be hard to prioritize what will attract users the most.

Does your original question need a tweak? Try it again.

How can the re-design of the Barefoot Artists website allow for a wider audience reach that creates a greater impact?
